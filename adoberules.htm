Downloadable Atlantian Rapier Rules



Here are three downloadable files containing the new Atlantian rapier rules as of the 0ctober, 2002 Acorn newsletter. The rules are broken up into three sections because of the formatting of the Red Tape which combines all combat rules into one large section.

Aedan

NOTE: To download, you may be able to simply click on the link. Otherwise, right-click and choose the "Save Link Target As..." option.


This set of rules are in Adobe Acrobat format.

  2. Conventions - part 2
  3. Rules of the List - part 3
