This is the badge for the original incarnation of the Award of the Academie d'Espee. Since signing the White Scarf treaty and changing the badge and trappings of this award, the original "swords and roses" has been taken over by Nathanial Sharpe as a personal badge that he has since given blanket permission to any member of the Academie to use.

This image was produced by Connor Levingstoune.
