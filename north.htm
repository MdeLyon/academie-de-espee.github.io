The North Wing - Articles from the Academie



Welcome to the North Wing. In this room are kept the numerous papers and articles written by members of the Academie d'Espee. Their scope is broad and many topics are covered. I hope that you enjoy them and find that which aids you in your pursuit of our art. Kindly drop a note to the author, giving your opinions and feedback. If unable to deliver it directly, simply leave the missive with me and I shall pass it on as I am able.

Aedan
